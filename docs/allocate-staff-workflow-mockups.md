## Responsive Breakpoints

### Desktop (> 1024px)
- Grid: 4-6 columns
- Sidebar: 300px (if applicable)
- Cards: Full detail view

### Tablet (768px - 1024px)
- Grid: 3-4 columns
- Sidebar: Collapsible or hidden
- Cards: Medium detail

### Mobile (< 768px)
- Grid: 2 columns
- Sidebar: Hidden (hamburger menu)
- Cards: Compact view
- Stack steps vertically (Wizard)
